# Naming & Copy Rule: "No AI" Policy

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Marketing Rule**: Do NOT use the word "AI" or "Artificial Intelligence" in user-facing text.
> **Reasoning**: To build trust based on math and logic, avoiding the "AI Hype" fatigue.
> **Enforcement**: strict replacement of terms.

## 1. Forbidden Terms (Blacklist)
| Forbidden | Replacement | Context |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **AI** | **Algorithm** / **Engine** | "Our Engine analyzes..." |
| **AI Analysis** | **Statistical Analysis** | "View Statistical Analysis" |
| **AI Prediction** | **Probability Model** | "Forecast based on Probability" |
| **Chatbot** | **Assistant** / **Navigator** | "Ask the Navigator" |
| **Machine Learning** | **Pattern Recognition** | "Advanced Pattern Recognition" |

## 2. Brand Voice
*   **Scientific**: "Calculated," "Verified," "Derived."
*   **Objective**: "Data suggests," "Historical trend indicates."
*   **Transparent**: "Based on RSI and MACD convergence."

## 3. Product Naming
*   (Bad) AI Insight -> (Good) **Market Insight** or **Data Perspective**
*   (Bad) AI Score -> (Good) **ChartMaster Score** or **Signal Strength**
